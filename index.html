I'm an aspiring software engineer & game developer, keen on exploring areas of game development, human-computer interaction & VR/AR. I'm a 3rd year B.Eng candidate studying Software Engineering + Game Design at McMasterU.

As a natural problem solver & designer, I wanted to pursue a career that would integrate & foster my arsenal of skills & passions. My interests in art, gaming & math kickstarted at a very early age. From here, my endeavor to make a mark in the gaming/design/tech industry was born. On the daily, I enjoy grinding through personal projects, spilling my art on paper, grooving to beats, reading lifestyle books & enlightening myself with anything new & interesting! I also like to game.
