I'm Vanessa.

I'm an aspiring software engineer & game developer, keen in exploring areas of game development, graphics optimization, art/design, artificiall intelligence, and VR/AR technology. I'm currently a third-year B.Eng candidate specializing in Software Engineering & Game Design at McMaster University.

As a natural problem solver and designer, I wanted to pursue a career that would integrate, align, and foster my arsenal of skills and passions. Software has been the perfect outlet for me to express my ideas and originality in challenging and creative ways. I hope to work on projects that will progress and enhance the way we interact with technology in the entertainment industry.

On the daily, I enjoy grinding through personal projects, spilling artsy stuff on paper, dancing/grooving to a beat, reading lifestyle/fiction books, and learning anything new and interesting. I also like to game.
