Welcome to my website - take a glimpse into who I am!

I'm an aspiring software engineer & game developer - to be receiving my B.Eng in Software Engineering & Game Design @ McMaster University. My focus is in the graphics / gaming industry. I'm also a major enthusaist for VR, MR, and AR technology, and hope to dive further into the world of human-computer interaction.

Let's rollback a bit. My passion for design and gaming began with my 4 year old self - doodling characters from my favourite anime shows (NARUTO - "You know it!"), and raging over why I couldn't beat that lvl 3 Jigglypuff in Smash Bros Melee. To this day, I still find all of my time indulged in art and gaming worthwhile.

I'm also a HUGE Fan of Math (one of the kids that got nominated to particpate in high school math contests). I particularly enjoy pattern-based problems!

These activities - and the niche that I'm currently in - allow me to foster and exercise my nature to create, to design, to innovate, and to problem solve.

My mantra: "It always seems impossible until it's done." - Nelson Mandela
